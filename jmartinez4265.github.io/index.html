About Yummit

Hi, we're Yummit! We're a local business that was first established in the Phoenix area. Since we're local, we know that Arizona can get really hot - especially when you're out hiking on the trails! For that reason, we got you covered. Our stores offer healthy, frozen yogurt that is gluten-free AND dairy-fat free. Come in and create your perfect peak with our mountain of flavors and toppings!

Local Business

We're proud to be a local business! Arizona is our home and we love to interact with our customers. Whether it is a complaint or a compliment, we love to hear from you! Every Sunday, 5% of all sales goes back into helping the community.

Healthy & Delicious

We pride ourselves on providing a healthy and delcious option to our customers. All of yogurts are free of gluten and dairy-fat. On top of that, we have rotating sorbet offerings that are vegan-friendly!

Self-serve

Staying affordable has always been a goal of ours. We believe we have stayed true to this goal by using a self-serve method. With the price of 50 cents an ounce, grab a cup and serve as little or as much as you want!

How it works — Simple as 1, 2, 3

1

Swirl it! Choose one or more multiple frozen yogurt flavors and serve as much as you like into your cup.

2

Top it! Choose from a number of available toppings to put on your frozen yogurt.

3

Weigh it! Put your cup full of frozen yogurt on the scale and pay accordingly to the weight in ounces (plus tax).
